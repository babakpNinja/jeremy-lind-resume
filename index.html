Profile

A creative leader who unites strategy, design, and execution — turning big visions into enduring brands and companies that move markets.

Jeremy Lind is a brand and marketing leader with more than a decade shaping how ambitious, venture-backed companies show up in the world. As Managing Director of Product & Brand Experience at West, he helps founders translate bold ideas into coherent brands, sharp positioning, and customer experiences that compound into growth.

His approach is deliberately hybrid — equal parts strategist, designer, and operator — grounded in the belief that brand is built across every moment of the customer journey, not just the logo.
